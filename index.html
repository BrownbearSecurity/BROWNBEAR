TOR

Tor, short for "The Onion Router," is a free and open-source software designed to enhance privacy and security on the internet. It works by directing internet traffic through a worldwide volunteer network of servers to conceal a user's location and usage from anyone conducting network surveillance or traffic analysis. Tor encrypts data multiple times and routes it through several randomly selected nodes (also known as relays) on the Tor network, thereby making it extremely difficult to trace back to the user.


Downloading Tor is a straightforward process:

Choose the appropriate version for your operating system (Windows, macOS, Linux, or Android).

Follow the installation instructions provided on the website.
